Top 5 Colombian Alternative Songs

Listening to several spotify playlists in the last months, we have reached the top 5 of the most important songs for Colombian alternative rock. Touch the images of the bands and you can listen to their recommended song on youtube. This page is thanks to the holberton project and its future signing Juan Felipe Pedraza.

The Petit Fellas were born on May 26, 2012 as an alternative music group that mixes Jazz, Funk, Hip Hop and Soul. "(It was born) in a poster they announced in a bar called Anonymous," said the group. It is made up of six young people: Nicolás Barragán, better known as Nicolai Fella, vocalist of the group; Sebastian Panesso, guitarist; Cesar Henao, drummer; Adrián Hidalgo, saxophonist; Nicolás Garzón, bassist; and Andrés Gómez, keyboard player. This is one of his most recent singles, "WHAT HAPPENED TO US?" click on the image to see his video on youtube.

Diamante Eléctrico is a Colombian rock band formed in 2012. They are made up of Juan Galeano on lead vocals and bass, Daniel Álvarez on guitar and Andee Zeta on drums, this group has participated in more than 250 presentations at important festivals National and international. Click on the image to see his video "BROKEN" on youtube.

GOLD is one of the many collaborations that the albun Buitres & Co de Diamante electrico have and on this occasion they join with Los Petit Fellas to show us all that we are gold. Click on the image to hear "gold" on youtube.

Superlitio is a rock band, with influences from funk, Caribbean music and electronic music, originating from Cali, Colombia. The group currently consists of Pedro Rovetto (Bass), Pipe Bravo (Keys, guitar and voice), Alejandro Lozano (Lead guitar) and Armando González (Drums). In this occasion we bring the theme that took you to the peak of your career "I hurt you" click on the image to see his video on youtube.

The Mills is a Colombian rock band formed in Bogotá with five members in 2007, by vocalist Álvaro Charry (Bako), guitarist Jorge Bello (Geogy), bassist Ramón Gutiérrez, keyboard player Diego Cáceres and drummer Diego Cadavid .1 The band's formation was influenced by alternative rock bands. They release their first debut album titled Babel, recorded at "Art & co Records Studios" and produced by Jorge Holguin Pyngwi and released on December 18, 2009. And this gem of a song "predatory love" is the best we have been given. Click on the image to see his video "PREDATORY LOVE" on youtube.
